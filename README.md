This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of sales, costs, profit, customer segmentation, regional performance, and order trends for SN Corp.
The dashboard transforms raw business data into actionable insights using interactive charts, KPIs, and filters.

Project Overview

The SN Corp Dashboard is designed to help business teams understand their performance across different dimensions:
Total Sales, Cost & Profit KPIs
Customer-level business performance
Sales distribution by country (Map View)
Regional quantity sales breakdown (Pie Chart)
Sub-Category level KPIs (Bar Chart)
Quarterly & Yearly order trends (Line Chart)
Interactive filters (Date, Ship Mode)

Key Insights
Highest overall sales volume ~748.5K, with profit ~365.8K.
Sub-categories such as Books, Chairs, and Appliances contribute the most to total sales.
Central region leads with the highest sales quantity (~55%).
Orders show a steady year-over-year increase, peaking in 2020.
Country-level sales reveal strong performance in West & Central Europe.
